
Program : In second year computer engineering class, group A student's play cricket, group B student's play badminton, group C student's play football.
Write a python to compute the following:
1. List of students who play both cricket and badminton
2. List of students who play either cricket or badminton but not both
3. Number of students who neither play cricket nor badminton
4. Number of students who play cricket and football but not badminton

def first(groupA,groupB):
    newlist=[]
    lena=len(groupA)
    lenb=len(groupB)
    
    for i in range(lena):
        for j in range(lenb):
            if(groupA[i]==groupB[j]):
                newlist.append(groupA[i])
                break
    print("List of students who play both cricket and badminton")
    print(newlist)
